STRATEGY_NAME: VortexSurge  

STRATEGY_DETAILS:  

**Key Strategy Components:**  
1. **Vortex Indicator (VI)**: Confirms trend direction and strength.  
   - **VI+ (Positive Vortex)**: Bullish trend confirmation.  
   - **VI- (Negative Vortex)**: Bearish trend confirmation.  
2. **Volume Surge**: Validates breakout strength (e.g., volume > 20-period moving average).  
3. **Bollinger Band Width (BBW)**: Measures volatility for exit signals (contraction below a threshold, e.g., BBW < 0.5).  

**Entry Rules:**  
1. **Long Entry**:  
   - VI+ crosses above VI- (bullish trend shift).  
   - Price breaks above a recent high (e.g., 20-period peak).  
   - Volume exceeds its 20-period moving average by 1.5x.  
2. **Short Entry**:  
   - VI- crosses above VI+ (bearish trend shift).  
   - Price breaks below a recent low (e.g., 20-period trough).  
   - Volume exceeds its 20-period moving average by 1.5x.  

**Exit Rules:**  
1. **Long Exit**:  
   - BBW contracts below threshold (e.g., BBW < 0.5).  
   - VI+ crosses below VI- (trend reversal).  
2. **Short Exit**:  
   - BBW contracts below threshold (e.g., BBW < 0.5).  
   - VI- crosses below VI+ (trend reversal).  

**Risk Management:**  
1. **Stop-Loss**:  
   - Long: Below the breakout candle’s low or 1.5x ATR (14-period).  
   - Short: Above the breakout candle’s high or 1.5x ATR (14-period).  
2. **Position Sizing**: Risk ≤ 1-2% of capital per trade.  
3. **Trailing Stop**: Adjust stop to breakeven after 1.5x risk-to-reward (e.g., 3R profit).  

**Required Indicators:**  
1. Vortex Indicator (VI) (default period: 14).  
2. Bollinger Bands (20-period, 2 StdDev) + BBW.  
3. Volume (20-period moving average).  
4. ATR (14-period) for stop placement.  

**Unique Edge:** Combines trend confirmation (VI) with volume-fueled breakouts and volatility-based exits, filtering false breakouts.
