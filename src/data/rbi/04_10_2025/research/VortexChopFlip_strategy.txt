STRATEGY_NAME: VortexChopFlip  

STRATEGY_DETAILS:  

**Key Strategy Components:**  
1. **Vortex Indicator (VI+ and VI-):** Identifies trend direction and potential reversals when VI+ crosses above/below VI-.  
2. **Choppiness Index (CI):** Measures market consolidation (values >61.8) or trending phases (values <38.2). Used to confirm low-volatility conditions before reversals.  
3. **ADX (14-period):** Filters trades by trend strength (ADX >25 for valid signals). Exits occur when ADX declines below 20.  
4. **Volume Confirmation:** Requires above-average volume on entry/exit to validate signals.  

**Entry Rules:**  
- **Long Entry:**  
  1. VI+ crosses **above** VI- (bullish reversal signal).  
  2. CI crosses **below** 61.8 (exiting choppy phase).  
  3. ADX >25 (trend strength confirmation).  
  4. Volume > 20-period average.  

- **Short Entry:**  
  1. VI+ crosses **below** VI- (bearish reversal signal).  
  2. CI crosses **above** 38.2 (entering choppy phase).  
  3. ADX >25.  
  4. Volume > 20-period average.  

**Exit Rules:**  
- **Profit-Taking:**  
  - Trail stop at 2x ATR (14-period) from peak/trough.  
- **Stop-Loss:**  
  - Fixed: 1.5x ATR from entry.  
- **Early Exit:**  
  - ADX drops below 20 (trend weakening).  
  - Volume falls below 20-period average.  

**Risk Management:**  
- Max risk per trade: 1-2% of capital.  
- No trades during major news events (high volatility skews CI/VI).  
- Avoid overlapping positions in correlated assets.  

**Required Indicators:**  
1. Vortex Indicator (VI+, VI-; default 14-period).  
2. Choppiness Index (14-period, range-bound).  
3. ADX (14-period).  
4. Volume (20-period MA).  
5. ATR (14-period for stops).  

**Unique Edge:**  
- Combines **trend reversal signals (VI)** with **volatility context (CI)** to avoid false breakouts.  
- Volume + ADX filters ensure high-probability entries/exits.  
- Optimized for swing trading (2-10 day holds).  

**Backtest Note:**  
- Test on assets with mean-reverting tendencies (e.g., indices, commodities). Avoid strongly trending cryptos.
