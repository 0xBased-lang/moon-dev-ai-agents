STRATEGY_NAME: VortexForce  

STRATEGY_DETAILS:  

**Key Strategy Components:**  
1. **Vortex Indicator (VI)**: Used to confirm trend direction (VI+ for bullish, VI- for bearish).  
2. **Elder Force Index (EFI)**: Combines price movement and volume to gauge momentum strength.  
3. **Threshold Alignment**: Requires both indicators to simultaneously confirm signals above/below predefined thresholds.  

**Entry Rules:**  
- **Long Entry**: Trigger when:  
  - VI+ crosses above VI- (bullish trend confirmation).  
  - EFI > +X threshold (positive momentum with volume backing).  
- **Short Entry**: Trigger when:  
  - VI- crosses above VI+ (bearish trend confirmation).  
  - EFI < -X threshold (negative momentum with volume backing).  

**Exit Rules:**  
- **Long Exit**: When EFI peaks and reverses below +Y threshold (momentum fade).  
- **Short Exit**: When EFI troughs and rises above -Y threshold (momentum rebound).  

**Risk Management:**  
1. **Stop-Loss**: Place below recent swing low (long) or above swing high (short).  
2. **Position Sizing**: Adjust based on volatility (e.g., ATR-based sizing).  
3. **Threshold Calibration**: Optimize X/Y values per asset (e.g., EFI +0.5/-0.5 for stocks, ±0.3 for forex).  

**Required Indicators:**  
1. Vortex Indicator (VI+, VI-).  
2. Elder Force Index (EFI) with 13-period EMA smoothing.  
3. Optional: ATR for volatility-adjusted stops.  

**Unique Edge:**  
- Combines trend persistence (Vortex) with volume-powered momentum (EFI), filtering false breakouts.  
- Exits at opposing EFI extremes capture reversals before price action confirms them.  

**Example Parameters (adjust per asset):**  
- VI Period: 14  
- EFI Period: 13 (EMA-smoothed)  
- EFI Entry Thresholds: ±0.5  
- EFI Exit Thresholds: ±0.2 (less strict than entry).
