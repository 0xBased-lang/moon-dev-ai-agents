STRATEGY_NAME: VortexDivergence  

STRATEGY_DETAILS:  

**Key Strategy Components:**  
1. **Vortex Indicator (VI):** Confirms the prevailing trend direction (uptrend if VI+ > VI-, downtrend if VI- > VI+).  
2. **Chande Momentum Oscillator (CMO):** Detects momentum divergence (bullish divergence in downtrends, bearish divergence in uptrends).  
3. **Volume Analysis:** Requires decreasing volume during divergence to validate weakening trend momentum.  
4. **Dynamic Trailing EMA Band:** Acts as exit trigger (price crossing EMA band signals trend resumption).  

**Entry Rules:**  
- **Counter-Trend Long Entry:**  
  1. VI- > VI+ (downtrend confirmed).  
  2. CMO shows higher low while price makes lower low (bullish divergence).  
  3. Volume decreases during divergence.  
- **Counter-Trend Short Entry:**  
  1. VI+ > VI- (uptrend confirmed).  
  2. CMO shows lower high while price makes higher high (bearish divergence).  
  3. Volume decreases during divergence.  

**Exit Rules:**  
- **Long Exit:** Price closes below the lower band of the dynamic trailing EMA (e.g., EMA-2%).  
- **Short Exit:** Price closes above the upper band of the dynamic trailing EMA (e.g., EMA+2%).  

**Risk Management:**  
- **Stop-Loss:** Place below the recent swing low (long) or above the swing high (short).  
- **Position Sizing:** Risk ≤1-2% of capital per trade.  
- **Filter:** Avoid trading during high-impact news events to prevent false signals.  

**Required Indicators:**  
1. Vortex Indicator (VI+, VI-).  
2. Chande Momentum Oscillator (CMO, default 14-period).  
3. Volume (confirming decreasing trend).  
4. Dynamic EMA Band (e.g., 20-period EMA ±2% bands).  

**Unique Edge:** Combines trend confirmation (VI), momentum reversal (CMO divergence), and volume decay for high-probability counter-trend setups, while the trailing EMA band ensures adaptive exits.
