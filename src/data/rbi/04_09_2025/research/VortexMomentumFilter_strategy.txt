STRATEGY_NAME: VortexMomentumFilter  

STRATEGY_DETAILS:  

**Key Strategy Components:**  
1. **Vortex Indicator (VI+ and VI-):** Identifies trend direction and potential reversals via crossover signals.  
2. **Chande Momentum Oscillator (CMO):** Confirms momentum strength (thresholds: +50 for bullish, -50 for bearish).  
3. **Volume Contraction Filter:** Requires volume to be below its 20-period moving average during consolidation.  

**Entry Rules (Long):**  
- VI+ crosses above VI- (bullish vortex signal).  
- CMO > +50 (confirming strong bullish momentum).  
- Volume must be below its 20-period MA (signaling consolidation before breakout).  

**Entry Rules (Short):**  
- VI- crosses above VI+ (bearish vortex signal).  
- CMO < -50 (confirming strong bearish momentum).  
- Volume must be below its 20-period MA (consolidation filter).  

**Exit Rules:**  
- **Profit-Taking:** Close position when CMO reverses past ±30 (momentum weakening).  
- **Stop-Loss:**  
  - Long: Below the recent swing low.  
  - Short: Above the recent swing high.  

**Risk Management:**  
- Position size: 1-2% of capital per trade.  
- Stop-loss must be placed immediately after entry.  
- Avoid trading during low-volume periods (holidays, overnight gaps).  

**Required Indicators:**  
1. Vortex Indicator (VI+, VI-; default 14-period).  
2. Chande Momentum Oscillator (CMO; 20-period).  
3. Volume 20-period moving average.  

**Unique Edge:** Combines trend-following (Vortex) with momentum confirmation (CMO) while filtering false breakouts via volume contraction—ideal for ranging-to-trending transitions.
