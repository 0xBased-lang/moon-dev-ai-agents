STRATEGY_NAME: VortexAccel  

STRATEGY_DETAILS:  

**Key Strategy Components:**  
1. **Vortex Indicator Crossover:** Uses VI+ (positive trend) and VI- (negative trend) lines to identify trend reversals.  
2. **Volume Acceleration:** Confirms entries with increasing volume (e.g., volume > 20-period SMA).  
3. **Volatility-Adjusted Stop-Loss:** Uses ATR (Average True Range) to set dynamic stop levels.  

**Entry Rules:**  
- **Long Entry:** VI+ crosses above VI- **AND** volume > 20-period SMA.  
- **Short Entry:** VI- crosses above VI+ **AND** volume > 20-period SMA.  

**Exit Rules:**  
- **Take-Profit:** 2x ATR (14-period) from entry.  
- **Stop-Loss:** 1.5x ATR (14-period) from entry.  

**Risk Management:**  
- Position sizing: 1-2% risk per trade.  
- No trades during low volatility (ATR < recent average).  

**Required Indicators:**  
1. Vortex Indicator (VI+, VI-)  
2. Volume (20-period SMA)  
3. ATR (14-period)  

This strategy combines trend confirmation (Vortex) with volume momentum, while using volatility to manage risk dynamically.
