STRATEGY_NAME: VolumetricMomentum  

STRATEGY_DETAILS:  

**Key Strategy Components:**  
1. **ADX (Average Directional Index):** Measures trend strength (threshold >25 for valid trends).  
2. **On-Balance Volume (OBV):** Identifies volume-driven divergence (price vs. OBV).  
3. **Keltner Channels:** Volatility-adjusted dynamic exit bands (20-period EMA Â± 2x ATR).  

**Entry Rules (Long Example):**  
- **Trend Confirmation:** ADX >25 *and* +DI > -DI (uptrend).  
- **Volume Divergence:** Price makes lower low, but OBV makes higher low (bullish divergence).  
- **Entry Trigger:** Price closes above prior swing high (confirms reversal).  

**Exit Rules:**  
- **Profit-Taking:** Close position when price touches upper Keltner band.  
- **Stop-Loss:** Lower Keltner band (adjusted daily via ATR).  
- **Emergency Exit:** ADX drops below 20 (trend weakness).  

**Risk Management:**  
- Position size: 1-2% of capital per trade.  
- Stop-loss distance: 1.5x recent ATR from entry.  
- No trades during major economic news (volatility spikes).  

**Required Indicators:**  
1. ADX (14-period)  
2. OBV  
3. Keltner Channels (20 EMA, 2x ATR multiplier)  

**Unique Edge:** Combines *volume confirmation* (OBV) with *adaptive exits* (Keltner + ATR), reducing false signals in choppy markets. Backtest with 1-hour+ timeframes for reliability.
