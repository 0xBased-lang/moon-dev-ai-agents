STRATEGY_NAME: KeltnerDivergence  

STRATEGY_DETAILS:  

**Key Strategy Components:**  
1. **Keltner Channel Breakout:** Uses a volatility-based channel to identify potential breakout opportunities.  
2. **Chaikin Money Flow (CMF) Divergence:** Detects opposing momentum signals when price breaks the Keltner Channel.  
3. **Elder-Ray Index:** Acts as the exit signal, confirming trend reversals.  

**Entry Rules:**  
- **Long Entry:**  
  - Price closes above the upper Keltner Channel.  
  - CMF shows a bearish divergence (price making higher highs while CMF makes lower highs).  
- **Short Entry:**  
  - Price closes below the lower Keltner Channel.  
  - CMF shows a bullish divergence (price making lower lows while CMF makes higher lows).  

**Exit Rules:**  
- **Long Exit:**  
  - Elder-Ray Index (Bull Power) turns negative (closes below zero).  
- **Short Exit:**  
  - Elder-Ray Index (Bear Power) turns positive (closes above zero).  

**Risk Management:**  
- **Stop Loss:**  
  - For long trades: Place below the recent swing low or lower Keltner Channel.  
  - For short trades: Place above the recent swing high or upper Keltner Channel.  
- **Position Sizing:** Risk no more than 1-2% of capital per trade.  

**Required Indicators:**  
1. **Keltner Channel** (20-period EMA, 2x ATR for bands).  
2. **Chaikin Money Flow (CMF)** (20-period).  
3. **Elder-Ray Index** (Bull Power & Bear Power).  

**Additional Notes:**  
- Works best in trending markets; avoid ranging conditions.  
- Confirm entries with higher timeframes for stronger signals.  
- Adjust ATR multiplier in Keltner Channel for more/less sensitivity.  

This strategy combines volatility-based breakouts with momentum divergence for high-probability trades, while using Elder-Ray for disciplined exits.
