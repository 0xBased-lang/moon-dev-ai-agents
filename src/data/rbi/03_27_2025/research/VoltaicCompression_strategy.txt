STRATEGY_NAME: VoltaicCompression  

STRATEGY_DETAILS:  
**Key Strategy Components:**  
1. **ADX (Average Directional Index)** - Measures trend strength (threshold: >25 for valid trend)  
2. **Bollinger Band Width** - Identifies volatility contraction (narrowing bands = low volatility)  
3. **Keltner Channels** - Defines dynamic exit points when price breaks against the trend  

**Entry Rules:**  
- **Long Entry:**  
  - ADX >25 (strong trend)  
  - Bollinger Band width (BBW) below its 20-period average (volatility compression)  
  - Price closes above the upper Bollinger Band  
- **Short Entry:**  
  - ADX >25 (strong trend)  
  - BBW below its 20-period average  
  - Price closes below the lower Bollinger Band  

**Exit Rules:**  
- **Long Exit:** Price closes below the lower Keltner Channel (against uptrend)  
- **Short Exit:** Price closes above the upper Keltner Channel (against downtrend)  

**Risk Management:**  
- Stop-loss:  
  - Long trades: Recent swing low or 1.5x ATR below entry  
  - Short trades: Recent swing high or 1.5x ATR above entry

**Required Indicators:**  
1. ADX (14-period)  
2. Bollinger Bands (20,2) + BBW calculation  
3. Keltner Channels (20, 1.5x ATR)  
4. Optional: ATR (14) for stop-loss refinement  

**Unique Edge:** Combines trend confirmation (ADX) with volatility cycles (BBW) for high-probability entries, while Keltner Channels prevent overstaying trades during reversals. Works best on 1H-4H timeframes in trending markets.
