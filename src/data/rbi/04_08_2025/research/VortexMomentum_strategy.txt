STRATEGY_NAME: VortexMomentum  

STRATEGY_DETAILS:  

**Key Strategy Components:**  
1. **Vortex Indicator (VI+ and VI-):** Used to confirm the prevailing trend direction.  
   - VI+ > VI- indicates bullish trend  
   - VI- > VI+ indicates bearish trend  
2. **Chande Momentum Oscillator (CMO):** Identifies overbought/oversold conditions.  
   - CMO > +50 signals overbought (potential short entry)  
   - CMO < -50 signals oversold (potential long entry)  
3. **Volume Spikes:** Confirms reversal signals when volume exceeds a 20-period moving average by 1.5x.  
4. **Dynamic ATR Trailing Stops:** Adjusts exit points based on volatility (3x ATR from recent extreme).  

**Entry Rules:**  
- **Long Entry:**  
  - VI+ crosses above VI- (bullish trend confirmation)  
  - CMO < -50 (oversold condition)  
  - Volume spike (1.5x above 20-period MA)  
- **Short Entry:**  
  - VI- crosses above VI+ (bearish trend confirmation)  
  - CMO > +50 (overbought condition)  
  - Volume spike (1.5x above 20-period MA)  

**Exit Rules:**  
- **Trailing Stop:** 3x ATR from the most recent swing high (for longs) or swing low (for shorts).  
- **Early Exit:** If VI reverses (VI- crosses above VI+ for longs or vice versa for shorts).  

**Risk Management:**  
- Position size limited to 1-2% of capital per trade.  
- Stop-loss initially set at 1.5x ATR from entry.  
- No trades during low volatility (ATR < 0.5% of price).  

**Required Indicators:**  
1. Vortex Indicator (VI+, VI-)  
2. Chande Momentum Oscillator (CMO)  
3. Volume Moving Average (20-period)  
4. Average True Range (ATR, 14-period)  

**Unique Aspects:**  
- Combines trend confirmation (Vortex) with momentum extremes (CMO) and volume validation.  
- Dynamic ATR trailing stops adapt to volatility for optimal exits.  
- Filters out low-probability trades via volume spikes and trend alignment.  

**Note:** Works best on 1H-4H timeframes for swing trading. Avoid ranging markets (use ADX > 25 to confirm trend strength).
