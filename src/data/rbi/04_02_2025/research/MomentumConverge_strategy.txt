STRATEGY_NAME: MomentumConverge  

STRATEGY_DETAILS:  

**Key Strategy Components:**  
1. **Chande Momentum Oscillator (CMO):** Measures momentum strength and direction (default 14-period).  
2. **SuperTrend:** Identifies trend direction (default settings: 10-period ATR, 3x multiplier).  
3. **Volume-Weighted Average Price (VWAP) Deviation:** Confirms entries with volume-backed price spikes (threshold: ±1.5% from VWAP).  
4. **Fibonacci Extensions:** Uses 1.618x and 2.618x of recent volatility range (ATR-based) for profit targets.  

**Entry Rules:**  
- **Long Entry:**  
  1. CMO crosses above 50 (bullish momentum).  
  2. Price crosses above SuperTrend (trend confirmation).  
  3. VWAP deviation spikes ≥1.5% above VWAP (volume-backed breakout).  
- **Short Entry:**  
  1. CMO crosses below −50 (bearish momentum).  
  2. Price crosses below SuperTrend (trend confirmation).  
  3. VWAP deviation drops ≤−1.5% below VWAP (volume-backed breakdown).  

**Exit Rules:**  
- **Take-Profit:**  
  - First target: 1.618x ATR of the entry candle’s range.  
  - Second target: 2.618x ATR (trail partial position).  
- **Stop-Loss:**  
  - Placed at opposite SuperTrend level or 1x ATR from entry, whichever is tighter.  

**Risk Management:**  
- Max risk per trade: 1–2% of capital.  
- No trades during low-volume periods (VWAP confirmation required).  
- Avoid entries if CMO is between ±25 (neutral zone).  

**Required Indicators:**  
1. Chande Momentum Oscillator (14-period).  
2. SuperTrend (10-period ATR, 3x multiplier).  
3. VWAP + deviation bands (±1.5%).  
4. Average True Range (ATR, 14-period) for Fibonacci extensions.  

**Unique Edge:** Combines momentum (CMO), trend (SuperTrend), and volume (VWAP) filters to avoid false breakouts, targeting volatility-based extensions for high-probability exits.
