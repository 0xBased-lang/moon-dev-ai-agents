STRATEGY_NAME: ChaikinTSI Breakout  

STRATEGY_DETAILS:  

**Key Strategy Components:**  
1. **Momentum Confirmation:** Uses Chaikin Money Flow (CMF) and True Strength Index (TSI) crossovers to validate momentum shifts.  
2. **Breakout Filter:** Donchian Channel width narrowing indicates consolidation before potential breakouts.  
3. **Exit Signal:** Parabolic SAR provides trailing stop-loss and trend-exit signals.  

**Entry Rules:**  
1. **CMF & TSI Alignment:**  
   - CMF must cross above zero (bullish momentum).  
   - TSI line (default 25-period) must cross above its signal line (13-period EMA of TSI).  
2. **Donchian Confirmation:**  
   - Donchian Channel width (20-period) must be narrowing (lower highs in width over last 5 bars).  
   - Price breaks above Donchian upper band (for long) or below lower band (for short).  

**Exit Rules:**  
1. **Parabolic SAR Reversal:** Close position when SAR dots flip to the opposite side of price.  
2. **Emergency Stop:** Fixed 2% below entry price (intra-trade risk cap).  

**Risk Management:**  
- Position size: 1-2% of capital per trade.  
- No entries if Donchian width is expanding (avoid false breakouts).  
- TSI must remain above signal line to hold position.  

**Required Indicators:**  
1. Chaikin Money Flow (20-period).  
2. True Strength Index (25-period TSI, 13-period signal).  
3. Donchian Channel (20-period width analysis).  
4. Parabolic SAR (default 0.02 step, 0.2 max).  

**Unique Edge:** Combines money flow (CMF) with smoothed momentum (TSI) and volatility contraction (Donchian) for high-probability breakouts, while SAR locks in profits dynamically. Avoids choppy markets via Donchian width filter.
