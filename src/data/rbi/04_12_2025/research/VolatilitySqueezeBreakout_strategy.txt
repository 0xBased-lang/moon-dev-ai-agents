STRATEGY_NAME: VolatilitySqueezeBreakout

STRATEGY_DETAILS:
This strategy combines Bollinger Bands and Keltner Channels to identify volatility squeeze conditions, then waits for confirmed breakouts with volume and ADX confirmation for high-probability trades.

Key Components:
1. Volatility Squeeze Detection:
   - Bollinger Bands (20,2)
   - Keltner Channel (20,1.5)
   - Squeeze occurs when Bollinger Bands are inside Keltner Channel

2. Breakout Confirmation:
   - Price closes outside Bollinger Bands
   - Volume exceeds 20-period average by 50%
   - ADX crosses above 25 (trend strength confirmation)

Entry Rules:
1. Long Entry:
   - Squeeze condition present (BB inside KC)
   - Price closes above upper Bollinger Band
   - Volume > 1.5x 20-period average volume
   - ADX > 25 and rising

2. Short Entry:
   - Squeeze condition present (BB inside KC)
   - Price closes below lower Bollinger Band
   - Volume > 1.5x 20-period average volume
   - ADX > 25 and rising

Exit Rules:
1. Trailing Stop:
   - Set at 2x recent volatility (ATR 14 period)
   - Adjusts with price movement in favorable direction
   - Exit when price touches trailing stop level

2. Time-based Exit:
   - Close position if no significant movement after 5 bars

Risk Management:
1. Position Sizing:
   - Risk no more than 1-2% of capital per trade
   - Calculate position size based on stop distance

2. Stop Placement:
   - Initial stop at opposite Bollinger Band
   - Moves to breakeven when price moves 1x ATR in favor

Required Indicators:
1. Bollinger Bands (20,2)
2. Keltner Channel (20,1.5)
3. ADX (14 period)
4. Volume (20-period average)
5. ATR (14 period for volatility measurement)

Additional Notes:
- Works best on 1H or 4H timeframes for swing trading
- Most effective in trending market conditions
- Avoid trading during news events that may cause false breakouts
- Combine with higher timeframe trend analysis for better results
