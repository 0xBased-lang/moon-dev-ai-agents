STRATEGY_NAME: FibonacciMACDDivergence

STRATEGY_DETAILS:
The strategy leverages MACD divergence as a key indicator for potential price reversals. Entries are triggered when the price breaks out beyond upper Bollinger Bands, combining with Fibonacci time zones to determine optimal exit points.

1. **Key Strategy Components**:
   - Utilizes MACD divergence to identify trend changes.
   - Breakout analysis using Bollinger Bands as a stop-loss mechanism.
   - Integration of Fibonacci retracement levels for potential profit targets.

2. **Entry Rules**:
   - Buy signals are generated when the price breaks above upper Bollinger Bands and MACD divergence is bullish (diverging upwards).
   - Confirmation with MACD histogram crossing above signal line.

3. **Exit Rules**:
   - Targets set at Fibonacci retracement levels for profit taking.
   - Stop-loss placed at the upper Bollinger Band to limit losses.

4. **Risk Management**:
   - Requires a risk-reward ratio of 1:2, ensuring potential gains are twice the stop-loss amounts.
   - Position sizing is adjusted based on account balance and volatility.
