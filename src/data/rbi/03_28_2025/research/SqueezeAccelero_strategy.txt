STRATEGY_NAME: SqueezeAccelero  

STRATEGY_DETAILS:  
**Key Strategy Components:**  
1. **Bollinger Band Squeeze Detection**: Identifies periods of low volatility when the bands contract significantly, often preceding strong breakouts.  
2. **Volume Rate-of-Change (ROC) Acceleration**: Confirms breakout validity by requiring volume momentum (3-day ROC) to exceed a predefined threshold, ensuring participation.  
3. **Breakout Entry**: Enters long/short positions when price breaks above/below the Bollinger Bands with volume confirmation.  
4. **Middle Band Exit**: Uses the middle band (typically a 20-period SMA) as a dynamic exit point for locking in profits or cutting losses.  

**Entry Rules:**  
- **Long Entry**: Triggered when:  
  1. Price closes *above* the upper Bollinger Band.  
  2. Volume ROC (3-day) exceeds the threshold (e.g., >20%).  
- **Short Entry**: Triggered when:  
  1. Price closes *below* the lower Bollinger Band.  
  2. Volume ROC (3-day) exceeds the threshold (e.g., >20%).  

**Exit Rules:**  
- **Long Exit**: Close position when price crosses *below* the middle Bollinger Band (20-period SMA).  
- **Short Exit**: Close position when price crosses *above* the middle Bollinger Band (20-period SMA).  

**Risk Management:**  
1. **Stop-Loss**: Optional fixed percentage (e.g., 2%) or ATR-based stop below/above recent swing low/high.  
2. **Position Sizing**: Allocate 1â€“2% of capital per trade to limit risk exposure.  
3. **Avoid Choppy Markets**: Filter trades using ADX (>25) to ensure trending conditions.  

**Required Indicators:**  
1. **Bollinger Bands** (20-period SMA, 2 standard deviations).  
2. **Volume ROC** (3-day rate-of-change).  
3. **Optional Confirmation**: ADX (14-period) for trend strength.  

**Unique Edge:** Combines volatility contraction (squeeze) with volume momentum, reducing false breakouts and capitalizing on high-probability moves.
