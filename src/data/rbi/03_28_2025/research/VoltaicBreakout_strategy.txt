STRATEGY_NAME: VoltaicBreakout  

STRATEGY_DETAILS:  
**Key Strategy Components:**  
1. **Volatility-Based Entry:** Uses Bollinger Bands to identify volatility expansion for potential breakouts.  
2. **Trend Strength Filter:** ADX > 30 ensures the breakout occurs in a strong trending market.  
3. **Overbought Exit:** RSI crossing below 70 signals weakening momentum for exit.  

**Entry Rules:**  
- Go **LONG** when:  
  - Price closes **above the upper Bollinger Band** (default 20-period, 2 StdDev).  
  - ADX (14-period) is **above 30**, confirming strong trend momentum.  

**Exit Rules:**  
- Exit the trade when:  
  - RSI (14-period) crosses **below 70**, indicating overbought conditions and potential reversal.  

**Risk Management:**  
1. **Stop-Loss:** Optional trailing stop (e.g., below recent swing low or lower Bollinger Band).

3. **Timeframe Suitability:** Best on 1H/4H/Daily charts for reduced noise.  

**Required Indicators:**  
1. **Bollinger Bands** (20, 2).  
2. **ADX** (14-period) +DI/-DI lines optional but recommended.  
3. **RSI** (14-period).  

**Unique Edge:** Combines volatility breakout (Bollinger Bands) with trend confirmation (ADX) and momentum exit (RSI), reducing false breakouts in choppy markets. Avoids short-side trades to focus on high-probability bullish setups.  

**Optimization Tips:**  
- Test ADX thresholds (e.g., 25-35) for different assets.  
- Adjust RSI exit level (e.g., 60 for aggressive traders).  
- Pair with volume confirmation for higher accuracy.
