STRATEGY_NAME: VortexEmergence  

STRATEGY_DETAILS:  

**Key Strategy Components:**  
1. **Vortex Indicator (VI+ and VI-):**  
   - Tracks directional movement and trend strength  
   - VI+ (positive trend movement) crossing above VI- (negative trend movement) signals potential bullish momentum  

2. **Choppiness Index (14-period):**  
   - Measures market consolidation/trending conditions  
   - Low values (<35) indicate trending markets (favorable for entries)  
   - High values (>65) indicate choppy/consolidating markets (exit signal)  

**Entry Rules:**  
- **Long Entry:** Triggered when:  
  1. VI+ crosses **above** VI- (bullish momentum)  
  2. Choppiness Index (14) is **below 35** (confirming trending conditions)  

**Exit Rules:**  
- **Long Exit:** Triggered when:  
  1. Choppiness Index (14) rises **above 65** (market entering consolidation)
